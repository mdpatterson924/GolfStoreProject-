Golf Tips

Get fit for new clubs. Kepp your head down. Maintain Balance from start to finish.

Setup like an outfileder; evenly balnched in a position of agility. Grip pressure is important, hold the club as tough it were a bird. A hard squeeze kills it but too light and it goes flying.

For best tee shot results, always remember to tee the ball with its northern hemesphere above the top of the driver.
